How to Reach:

DHARAMSALA BY AIR

From Delhi Airport, which is connected to all major cities in India and abroad, you fly to Dharamsala domestic airport with Air India or SpiceJet.

DHARAMSALA BY TRAIN

The nearest train station is Chakki Bank in Pathankot, which is about 85 km away from Dharamsala. Chakki Bank station is well connected to all major cities in India. After arriving in Pathankot you could either take a taxi to Dharamsala which is about 2 hours traveling time or bus which takes about 3,5 hours. List or trains form Delhi to Pothankot and Pathankot to Delhi Attached are two tables which gives complete info of trains with their departure and arrival at respected stations running in between New Delhi/ Delhi and Pathan / Chakki Bank. As you are aware New Delhi ( NDSL ) or Delhi ( DLI ) are two train stations of same city and same is the case of Pathankot ( PTK ) Chakki Bank( CHKB ). One train also originates from Anand Vihar ( ANVT ) another train station added to Delhi city.

DHARAMSALA BY BUS

Regular direct bus services are available to Dharamsala from many cities. From Delhi (520 km), there are both government and private luxury buses available at about Rs. 1000.

GUIDE FROM DELHI AIRPORT TO DELHI BUS STATION

When you come out of the arrival gate of the T3 Delhi airport you will find sign boards indicating the underground metro station on the right hand side. This is the airport express link of the Delhi Metro system. Get on the metro and get of on the last station - New Delhi Railway Station. It will cost you approx. Rs 100 and take around 19 minutes. Then ask someone for the way to the main metro station where you can buy a ticket for the station called Kashmiri Gate. This is the ISBT (Inter State Bus Terminal) where you can get various buses to come to Dharamshala. Most people travel by the AC Volvo leaving at 8.30 pm and arriving in Dharamshala at 6.30 am. You can also book in advance the bus ticket at www.hp.gov/hrtctickets/ You can also look at the Delhi Metro map if you like - www.delhimetrorail.com/zoom-route-map.aspx
