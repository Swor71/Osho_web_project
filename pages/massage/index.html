About Massage

Massage - For people who love themselves.

Receiving massage is an important part of a seekers journey. In a combination with meditation techniques, it gives a great support to your growth process. It helps to make the body purified, the life energy flowing and your whole system more tuned. Then you can more easily tune with nature and existence, and flow with the river of life.

Because of stiffness and rigidness in the body, many cannot do the active meditations created by Osho in the way they are supposed to be done. Especially Dynamic Meditation is difficult to do with the energy and totality which is required. Massage helps to give more flexibility, it loosens up rigid points and resistant parts of your body, softly dissolving emotional blockages so to give you a foundation for the Active meditations and prepare you to go deeper into meditation.

"Massage can give such a glimpse into spirituality"
