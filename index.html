About Ayurveda

Ayurveda - A science for inner growth. Our approach to Ayurveda is going to the roots of its science where the basic idea is to prepare a ground for spiritual growth. We would say Ayurveda is the Art of living life with harmony and healthy function of the body and mind. Only when this harmony is attained there is space for the soul to grow and a chance to attain harmony with cosmos. According Ayurveda there are five cosmic forces in nature: Earth, Water, Fire, Air and Ether. Certain combinations of them make three forces called Dosha: Vatta, Pitta, Kapha. Each of them resides in the body and has certain responsibilities, controlling physical and mental functions. The proportions of the Dosha are different to everyone and this is the body’s true nature and creates our individuality.
